About Me


I am a first year Mathematics PhD student at the University of Manchester under the supervision of Omar León Sánchez. My interests lie in model theory, category theory and noncommutative algebra. In particular, I am interested in:
  • functorial appproaches to the model theory of modules
  • stability theory for accessible categories
  • model-theoretic classification problems
  • applications of model theory to representation theory and algebraic geometry
I completed my MSc dissertation here in Manchester under the supervision of Rose Wagstaffe. The topic was the model theory of modules, giving an introductory exposition to the use of functorial methods, the Ziegler spectrum and elementary dualities. The MSc dissertation was preceded by a "Main Project" which covers much of the important background material. You can read them here:
  • Main Project: An Equivalence of Functorial and Model-Theoretic perspectives on Modules (link)
  • Dissertation: Torsion Theory, Elementary Duality and their applications to the Ziegler Spectrum (link)
